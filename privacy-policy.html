Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026/06/30

This Privacy Policy explains what information Invoice Maker: Deposit & Bill ("the App") collects, how it is stored, and how it is used. The App is developed by Yuan Chen ("we," "us," or "our").

1. Summary

The App is designed so that, by default, almost nothing leaves your device or your own iCloud account. We do not run our own servers that store your business data. We do not use advertising. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking software.

2. What Information the App Stores

To provide its core functionality, the App stores the following information, which you enter yourself:

  • Business profile information: your business name, logo, contact email, contact phone number, default payment instructions, and default pricing preferences.
  • Client information: names, email addresses, phone numbers, and notes for clients you add.
  • Project and invoice data: project titles, event dates, line items, prices, tax settings, invoice statuses, and payment notes you record (e.g. marking an invoice as paid).
  • Templates: any reusable service/pricing templates you create.

3. Where This Information Is Stored

All of the information described above is stored locally on your device and synced using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit private database), under your own Apple ID. This means:

  • Your data is stored in the private iCloud container associated with your own Apple ID, not on a server we operate.
  • We do not have our own backend server for this data, and we cannot access, view, or retrieve your business, client, or invoice information.
  • Syncing across your own devices (e.g. an iPhone and an iPad signed into the same Apple ID) happens through Apple's iCloud infrastructure, governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.
  • If you delete the App, or disable iCloud for the App in your device settings, locally stored data may be removed in accordance with standard iOS behavior; data already synced to your private iCloud container is controlled by your Apple ID's iCloud settings.

4. Purchases and Payment Information

The App offers optional subscriptions and a one-time consumable purchase, processed entirely through Apple's App Store (StoreKit). We do not collect, see, transmit, or store your credit card number, billing address, or any other payment details. All payment processing is handled directly by Apple and governed by Apple's own privacy and payment terms. We only receive confirmation from Apple's systems that a purchase was completed, which we use locally on your device to unlock the relevant features.

5. Advertising and Tracking

The App does not contain any advertising. The App does not use any third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or data brokers, because we do not collect it on any server in the first place.

6. Notifications

The App may schedule local reminder notifications (for example, a reminder to send a balance invoice before an upcoming event date). These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device using Apple's standard notification system. No notification content or scheduling data is sent to us or to any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by business owners and professionals (such as photographers) and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction).

8. Your Rights and Choices

Because your business, client, and invoice data lives in your own private iCloud account rather than on our servers, you are already in direct control of it:

  • You can view, edit, or delete any client, project, or invoice record directly within the App at any time.
  • You can delete all App data by deleting the App and removing its data from your iCloud storage settings (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage on your device).
  • If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, or another jurisdiction with specific data protection rights, you may have additional rights regarding any limited information we do process (such as purchase confirmations relayed by Apple). To ask a question about this, contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add a new feature that changes what information the App stores. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes are made. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
